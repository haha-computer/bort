# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** January 2026

Bort is a small experimental Discord bot. Here's what we do (and don't do) with your data.

## What we collect

When you interact with Bort, we process:

- Your Discord username (to identify who's talking)
- Your message content (to respond to you)
- Recent channel history (last 10 messages, for conversation context)

## How we use it

Your messages are sent to OpenAI's API to generate responses. That's it. We don't:

- Store your messages in our own database
- Sell your data to anyone
- Use your data for training or analytics
- Track you across servers

## Third parties

We use these services:

- **Discord** — Messages go through Discord's platform. See [Discord's Privacy Policy](https://discord.com/privacy).
- **OpenAI** — Your messages are sent to OpenAI to generate responses. See [OpenAI's Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/privacy).
- **Fly.io** — The bot runs on Fly.io's infrastructure. See [Fly.io's Privacy Policy](https://fly.io/legal/privacy-policy).

## Data retention

We don't persist your messages. They exist only for the duration of the API call to OpenAI. Logs may temporarily contain error information but are not retained long-term.

OpenAI and Discord have their own retention policies — check their privacy policies for details.

## Your rights

Want your data deleted? We don't store it, so there's nothing to delete on our end. For Discord or OpenAI, contact them directly.

## Contact

Questions? Find us in the Discord server.
